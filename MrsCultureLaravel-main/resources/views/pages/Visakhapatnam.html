Welcome to Visakhapatnam!

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is a coastal city in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is known for its pristine beaches, serene hills, and rich cultural heritage. With a perfect blend of natural beauty and modernity, Visakhapatnam offers a delightful experience to its visitors.

Explore the breathtaking views from Kailasagiri Hill, relax at the beautiful Rishikonda Beach, and visit the famous Submarine Museum. Don't miss the chance to savor the local delicacies and experience the warm hospitality of the people.

Popular Places to Visit in Visakhapatnam

Rishikonda Beach

Rishikonda Beach is a scenic beach known for its golden sands, clear blue waters, and adventurous water sports. It is a popular spot for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying the natural beauty of the coastline.

Location
Submarine Museum

The Submarine Museum is a unique museum located inside a decommissioned submarine. It offers an insight into the Indian Navy's submarine operations and history. Visitors can explore the submarine and learn about its workings.

Location
Simhachalam Temple

The Simhachalam Temple is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Narasimha, an avatar of Lord Vishnu. It is known for its exquisite architecture and spiritual significance. Devotees visit the temple to seek blessings and experience the divine atmosphere.
