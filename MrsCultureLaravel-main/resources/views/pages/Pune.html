Welcome to Pune!

Pune, the cultural capital of Maharashtra, is a vibrant city known for its historical significance, educational institutions, and diverse culture. It is a blend of tradition and modernity, offering a unique experience to visitors.

Explore the rich heritage of Pune by visiting iconic landmarks like Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace, and the beautiful hill forts of Sinhagad and Rajgad. Experience the lively street markets, indulge in mouth-watering street food, and witness the city's thriving arts and music scene.

Popular Places to Visit in Pune

Aga Khan Palace

Aga Khan Palace is a majestic palace that holds historical significance. It is known for its beautiful architecture, sprawling gardens, and being a tribute to the Indian freedom struggle.

Location
Sinhagad Fort

Sinhagad Fort is a popular destination for trekking enthusiasts. It offers breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and provides insights into the historic battles fought in the region.

Location
Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple

Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple is a famous temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha. It is known for its grandeur, religious significance, and the majestic idol of Lord Ganesha.
