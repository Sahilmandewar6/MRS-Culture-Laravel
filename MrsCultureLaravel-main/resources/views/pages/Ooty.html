Welcome to Ooty!

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station located in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, India. With its pleasant weather, scenic beauty, and colonial charm, Ooty has earned the title of "Queen of Hill Stations."

Explore the lush green landscapes, picturesque valleys, and cascading waterfalls. Enjoy a ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway, visit the tea gardens, and indulge in adventure activities like trekking and boating. Ooty offers a perfect retreat for nature lovers and those seeking tranquility.

Popular Tourist Places in Ooty

Doddabetta Peak

Doddabetta Peak is the highest peak in the Nilgiri Hills, offering panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and forests. Visit the telescope house at the peak for a closer look at the breathtaking landscapes.

Location
Ooty Lake

Ooty Lake is a man-made lake surrounded by lush greenery. Enjoy boating in the tranquil waters, go for a leisurely walk along the lakeside, or simply relax and admire the scenic beauty of the surroundings.

Location
Botanical Garden

The Botanical Garden in Ooty is a well-maintained garden featuring a wide variety of exotic plants, flowers, and trees. Take a leisurely stroll amidst the colorful blooms and enjoy the tranquility of the garden.
