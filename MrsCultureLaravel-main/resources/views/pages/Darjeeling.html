Welcome to Darjeeling!

Darjeeling, often referred to as the "Queen of Hills," is a charming hill station located in West Bengal, India. Situated in the Lesser Himalayas at an elevation of 6,710 feet (2,045 meters), Darjeeling offers breathtaking views of the snow-capped Kanchenjunga, the third highest peak in the world.

Explore the lush tea gardens, take a ride on the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, visit the monasteries, and enjoy the serene ambiance of this hill town. Darjeeling is renowned for its pleasant weather, colonial architecture, and the famous Darjeeling tea, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and tea enthusiasts.

Popular Tourist Places in Darjeeling

Tiger Hill

Tiger Hill is famous for its panoramic view of the sunrise over the snow-capped Himalayas. It offers breathtaking views of the Kanchenjunga peak and other nearby mountains. It's a must-visit spot to witness the mesmerizing beauty of the sunrise.

Location
Batasia Loop

Batasia Loop is a famous railway loop located on the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway. It offers a magnificent view of the surrounding mountains, including the Kanchenjunga. Enjoy a ride on the toy train and marvel at the engineering marvel of this spiral railway track.

Location
Tea Gardens

Darjeeling is renowned for its tea gardens that produce the world-famous Darjeeling tea. Visit the tea estates, take a tour of the tea factories, and learn about the tea-making process. Enjoy the picturesque views of the tea gardens and indulge in a cup of freshly brewed tea.
