Welcome to Agra!

Agra, located in the state of Uttar Pradesh, India, is famous for its rich Mughal heritage and architectural marvels. It is home to the magnificent Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

Explore the grandeur of the Mughal era by visiting Agra Fort, Itmad-ud-Daulah's Tomb, and Fatehpur Sikri. Immerse yourself in the history, culture, and beauty of this enchanting city.

Popular Places to Visit in Agra

Agra Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Agra Fort is a massive fortification built with red sandstone and known for its impressive architecture and historical significance.

Location
Itmad-ud-Daulah's Tomb

Also known as the "Baby Taj," this beautiful tomb is an architectural marvel made of white marble and is often considered a draft of the Taj Mahal.

Location
Fatehpur Sikri

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Fatehpur Sikri is a magnificent city built by Emperor Akbar that showcases the blend of Mughal and Persian architecture.
