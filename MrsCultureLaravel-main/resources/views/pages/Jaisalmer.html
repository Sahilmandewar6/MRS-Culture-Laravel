Welcome to Jaisalmer!

Jaisalmer, known as "The Golden City," is a captivating destination in the state of Rajasthan, India. It is famous for its majestic forts, stunning palaces, and unique golden sandstone architecture.

Explore the magnificent Jaisalmer Fort, visit the beautiful Patwon Ki Haveli, and experience the charm of the Gadisar Lake. Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage and warm hospitality of this desert city.

Popular Places to Visit in Jaisalmer

Patwon Ki Haveli

Visit the intricate Patwon Ki Haveli, a cluster of five beautifully carved havelis showcasing exquisite architecture and intricate artwork.

Location
Gadisar Lake

Take a serene boat ride on Gadisar Lake, surrounded by temples and beautiful architecture. Enjoy the mesmerizing views of the lake at sunset.

Location
Sam Sand Dunes

Experience the magic of the Thar Desert by visiting the Sam Sand Dunes. Enjoy camel rides, cultural performances, and witness the stunning desert sunset.
