Welcome to Pondicherry!

Pondicherry, also known as Puducherry, is a charming coastal town on the southeastern coast of India. It is known for its unique blend of French and Indian influences, evident in its architecture, cuisine, and culture.

Explore the serene beaches, stroll through the French Quarter with its colonial buildings, visit the Aurobindo Ashram for spiritual rejuvenation, and savor the delectable French and South Indian cuisine. Pondicherry offers a perfect getaway for a peaceful and cultural experience.

Popular Tourist Places in Pondicherry

Promenade Beach

Promenade Beach, also known as the Rock Beach, is a popular seafront promenade in Pondicherry. Enjoy a leisurely walk along the sandy beach, witness beautiful sunrises and sunsets, and indulge in beachside activities.

Location
Auroville

Auroville, also known as the City of Dawn, is an experimental township dedicated to human unity and spiritual growth. Visit the Matrimandir, a stunning golden spherical temple, and explore the lush green surroundings and meditation centers.

Location
Aurobindo Ashram

Aurobindo Ashram is a spiritual community founded by Sri Aurobindo and his spiritual collaborator, The Mother. Experience the peaceful ambiance, participate in meditation sessions, and explore the library and art gallery.
