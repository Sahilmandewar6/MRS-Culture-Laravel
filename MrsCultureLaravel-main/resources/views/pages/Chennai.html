Welcome to Chennai!

Chennai, the capital of Tamil Nadu, is a vibrant city located on the Bay of Bengal. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and stunning beaches. Chennai serves as the gateway to South India, offering a blend of tradition and modernity.

Explore the magnificent temples, experience the vibrant local markets, savor the delicious South Indian cuisine, and relax on the pristine beaches. Chennai is also famous for its traditional arts, music, and dance forms such as Bharatanatyam.

Popular Tourist Places in Chennai

Mahabalipuram

Mahabalipuram, also known as Mamallapuram, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its ancient temples and rock-cut monuments. Visit the Shore Temple, Pancha Rathas, and the intricately carved Arjuna's Penance.

Location
Marina Beach

Marina Beach is one of the longest urban beaches in the world. Take a leisurely stroll along the promenade, enjoy local street food, and witness mesmerizing sunsets. Don't miss the iconic lighthouse and the statues of Tamil literary figures.

Location
Kapaleeshwarar Temple

Kapaleeshwarar Temple is a renowned Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. It features Dravidian architecture with intricate carvings and colorful gopurams. Experience the spiritual ambience and witness the rituals and ceremonies.
