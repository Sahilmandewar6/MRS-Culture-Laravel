Gwalior - The Historic City

Gwalior, located in the central Indian state of Madhya Pradesh, is a city steeped in rich history and culture. It is famous for its magnificent forts, beautiful palaces, and ancient temples. Gwalior Fort, situated atop a hill, offers breathtaking views of the city.

Visit the historic Jai Vilas Palace, known for its stunning architecture and art collection. Explore the intricately carved Sas-Bahu Temples and the iconic Teli Ka Mandir. Gwalior is also known for its vibrant music scene, with the Gwalior Gharana being one of the most renowned classical music traditions.

Popular Places to Visit in Gwalior

Gwalior Fort

Explore the majestic Gwalior Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Admire the stunning architecture, visit the palaces, temples, and the iconic Man Mandir.

Jai Vilas Palace

Visit the grand Jai Vilas Palace, a magnificent blend of European and Indian architectural styles. Marvel at the opulent interiors and the Scindia family's art collection.

Sas Bahu Temples

Admire the intricate carvings and architectural beauty of the Sas Bahu Temples, dedicated to Lord Vishnu. These twin temples are a marvel of medieval Indian art and craftsmanship.
