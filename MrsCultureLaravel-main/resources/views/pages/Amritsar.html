Welcome to Amritsar!

Amritsar, the spiritual and cultural hub of Punjab, is home to the famous Golden Temple and holds significant historical importance. The city offers a blend of spirituality, history, and Punjabi hospitality.

Visit the GAolden Temple, experience the grandeur of the Wagah Border ceremony, explore the historical Jallianwala Bagh, and indulge in the delicious Punjabi cuisine. Amritsar promises a memorable and enriching experience for every traveler.

Popular Tourist Places in Amritsar

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, is the holiest Gurdwara and the spiritual center of Sikhism. It is a symbol of equality, devotion, and peace. Experience the serenity and architectural beauty of this magnificent shrine.

Location
Wagah Border

Wagah Border is the international border between India and Pakistan, known for its daily ceremonial closing ceremony. Witness the vibrant parade, patriotic fervor, and the lowering of flags, creating an electrifying atmosphere.

Location
Jallianwala Bagh

Jallianwala Bagh is a public garden that witnessed a tragic incident during India's struggle for independence. Pay homage to the martyrs who lost their lives in the Jallianwala Bagh massacre and explore the museum to learn about its history.
