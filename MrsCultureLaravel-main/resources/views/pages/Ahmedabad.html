Welcome to Ahmedabad!

Ahmedabad, the largest city in the state of Gujarat, is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical monuments, and vibrant street life. It is a perfect blend of tradition and modernity.

Explore the UNESCO World Heritage Site of the historic city center, visit the Sabarmati Ashram where Mahatma Gandhi lived, and indulge in the mouthwatering local cuisine. Ahmedabad has something to offer to every traveler.

Popular Places to Visit in Ahmedabad

Sidi Saiyyed Mosque

Sidi Saiyyed Mosque is a beautiful example of Indo-Islamic architecture known for its intricately carved stone lattice screens. It is a popular tourist attraction and an important historical monument in Ahmedabad.

Location
Sabarmati Riverfront

The Sabarmati Riverfront is a waterfront promenade developed along the banks of the Sabarmati River. It offers beautiful views, recreational activities, and is a popular spot for locals and tourists alike.

Location
Jama Masjid

Jama Masjid, located in the old city area of Ahmedabad, is one of the oldest and most revered mosques in the city. It showcases stunning architecture and is a significant religious site for the Muslim community.
