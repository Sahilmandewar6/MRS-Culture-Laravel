Welcome to Kochi!

Kochi, also known as Cochin, is a vibrant city located on the southwest coast of India. As the "Queen of the Arabian Sea," Kochi has a rich history influenced by various cultures including Portuguese, Dutch, and British. The city is famous for its bustling seaport, breathtaking backwaters, and spice trading history.

Explore the charming colonial architecture, visit ancient forts, indulge in delicious seafood, and experience the mesmerizing Kathakali dance performances. Kochi is also a gateway to the picturesque Kerala backwaters, serene beaches, and the famous hill station of Munnar.

Popular Tourist Places in Kochi

Fort Kochi

Fort Kochi is a historical neighborhood known for its colonial charm. Explore the iconic Chinese fishing nets, stroll along the picturesque promenade, and visit the beautiful St. Francis Church and Santa Cruz Basilica.

Location
Mattancherry Palace

Mattancherry Palace, also known as the Dutch Palace, is a museum showcasing Kerala's traditional art and architecture. Admire the exquisite murals, royal artifacts, and a vast collection of antiques.

Location
Willingdon Island

Willingdon Island is a man-made island known for its commercial activities and luxurious hotels. Enjoy a leisurely walk along the waterfront, indulge in waterfront dining, and witness the bustling port activities.
