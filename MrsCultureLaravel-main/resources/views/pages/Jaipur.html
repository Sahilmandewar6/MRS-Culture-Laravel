Welcome to Jaipur!

Jaipur, also known as the Pink City, is the capital of the royal state of Rajasthan in India. It is a city rich in history, culture, and architectural wonders. Jaipur is famous for its majestic palaces, vibrant markets, and traditional Rajasthani hospitality.

Explore the mesmerizing beauty of Jaipur by visiting iconic landmarks such as the Hawa Mahal, Amer Fort, and Jantar Mantar. Immerse yourself in the regal atmosphere, indulge in traditional Rajasthani cuisine, and shop for exquisite handicrafts.

Popular Tourist Places in Jaipur

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal, also known as the Palace of Winds, is a stunning pink sandstone palace with intricate latticework. It was built for the royal women to observe street festivities while maintaining their privacy. Visit Hawa Mahal to marvel at its unique architecture and enjoy panoramic views of the city.

Location
Amer Fort

Amer Fort, located on a hilltop, is a magnificent fort known for its impressive architecture and grandeur. Explore the ornate palaces, intricate carvings, and breathtaking views of Maota Lake. Don't miss the enchanting sound and light show held in the evening.

Location
Jantar Mantar

Jantar Mantar is an astronomical observatory built by Maharaja Jai Singh II. It features a collection of unique architectural instruments used to study the movement of celestial bodies. Visit Jantar Mantar to witness the fascinating precision and mathematical genius of ancient Indian astronomers.
